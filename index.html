About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage!
I'm currently a PhD student at the University of Bergamo, where I focus on statistical analysis and data science.
Here you can find the results of my research, along with educational materials for courses I offer at the university.

Why this site?

The main goals of this site are:
  • share my work with the statistics community
  • keep my code transparent and up-to-date
  • create a hub for the teaching materials
  • develop a clean framework for my previous and future jobs
I will continue to update this page regularly with new research insights, teaching resources, and any other relevant developments.
If you need clarification or have any questions about my work, feel free to reach out through the Contact section of this page.
I’m always happy to connect with others who share an interest in statistics and data science.
